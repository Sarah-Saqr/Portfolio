Projects

Your Global Trip

Your Global Trip is a modern travel booking app designed for YourGlobalTrip.com, helping users explore and book trips across six cities in Egypt. The app provides a seamless and intuitive experience, allowing travelers to discover destinations, customize itineraries, and book accommodations with ease.

Design Approach:

I focused on a minimalist, light-themed UI with an orange color scheme, clean typography, and smooth navigation. The design emphasizes usability, ensuring a frictionless experience for users planning their journeys.

Key Features:

  • City-Based Travel Planning – Explore curated trips in six Egyptian cities
  • Intuitive Navigation – Easy-to-use interface for seamless booking
  • Modern & Responsive UI – Designed for a visually engaging and accessible experience
  • User-Centric Approach – Research-driven design for maximum usability

Impact:

This project enhances the travel experience by simplifying trip planning while maintaining a visually engaging and functional design.

AALAA Designs

AALAA Designs is a modern e-commerce website for a fashion brand based in Egypt, specializing in women's clothing. The platform offers a seamless shopping experience, allowing customers to browse collections, explore styles, and purchase effortlessly.

Design Approach:

I crafted a clean, elegant, and user-friendly interface that reflects the brand's aesthetic. The design ensures a smooth shopping journey with intuitive navigation, engaging visuals, and a responsive layout for both desktop and mobile users.

Key Features:

  • Visually Engaging UI – Aesthetic design aligned with fashiontrends
  • Seamless Shopping Experience – Easy browsing, filtering, and checkout process
  • Mobile-First Approach – Fully responsive for a smooth mobile experience
  • Brand Identity Integration – Elegant typography and color scheme to enhance branding

Impact:

This project enhances online shopping for AALAA Designs, creating a stylish and functional e-commerce platform that elevates the brand's digital presence.
