Your Global Trip

Your Global Trip is a modern travel booking app designed for YourGlobalTrip.com, helping users explore and book trips across six cities in Egypt. The app provides a seamless and intuitive experience, allowing travelers to discover destinations, customize itineraries, and book accommodations with ease.

Design Approach:

I focused on a minimalist, light-themed UI with an orange color scheme, clean typography, and smooth navigation. The design emphasizes usability, ensuring a frictionless experience for users planning their journeys.

Key Features:

  • City-Based Travel Planning – Explore curated trips in six Egyptian cities
  • Intuitive Navigation – Easy-to-use interface for seamless booking
  • Modern & Responsive UI – Designed for a visually engaging and accessible experience
  • User-Centric Approach – Research-driven design for maximum usability

Impact:

This project enhances the travel experience by simplifying trip planning while maintaining a visually engaging and functional design.
